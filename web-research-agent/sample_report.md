## Findings
- EU AI Act News 2026 — Critical Updates & Enforcement Guide
  - Here is a concise summary of the text:
The EU AI Act is a regulation that sets out a complete legal framework for the development, deployment, and use of artificial intelligence (AI) in the European Union. The regulation has been in effect since February 2025 and will become fully applicable on August 2, 2026. The Act classifies every AI system by risk level before it can be put into use, and businesses that deploy AI in high-stakes contexts such as hiring, healthcare, and law enforcement must meet strict compliance requirements. The regulation applies to businesses outside of Europe if their AI systems affect people in the EU. ([1])
